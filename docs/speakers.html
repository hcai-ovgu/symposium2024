Meet our Speakers

Marko Tkalcic

Faculty of Mathematics, Natural Sciences and Information Technologies
at the University of Primorska in Koper, Slovenia

Dr. Marko Tkalčič is an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Mathematics, Natural Sciences and Information Technologies (FAMNIT), University of Primorska, Koper, Slovenia. His research focuses on enhancing personalized services, particularly recommender systems, by incorporating psychological models into personalization algorithms.

Marco Polignano

Professor Assistant in the Computer Science Department.
at the University of Bari Aldo Moro, Italy

Ph.D. Marco Polignano specializes in AI, NLP, and Recommender Systems as a member of the SWAP research group. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Mathematics.

Markus Schedl

Professor at Johannes Kepler University (JKU) Linz, Austria.

Dr. Markus Schedl is a Full Professor at Johannes Kepler University (JKU) Linz, leading the Multimedia Mining and Search group and the Human-centered Artificial Intelligence group. His research focuses on recommender systems, information retrieval, algorithmic fairness, machine learning, and data analysis.

Mantello Peter A

Professor at Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University

Dr. Peter A. Mantello specializes in the intersection of technology, media, and security. His research examines the feedback loops and interplay between AI, popular culture, security, and conflict. Dr. Mantello is the founder of ethikal.ai, a research lab dedicated to designing human-centric frameworks and tools for intelligent machines.

Raimund Dachselt

Professor at Technische Universität Dresden

Raimund Dachselt is the head of the Interactive Media Lab Dresden (Professorship of Multimedia Technology) at the Faculty of Computer Science of the Technische Universität Dresden
